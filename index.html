Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does Frat Alert work in?

+

Frat Alert currently works in major college towns across the United States. We're constantly expanding our coverage. Contact us to check if your area is supported.

How does the AI transcription work?

+

Our proprietary AI technology listens to local police scanners and other relevant audio sources, transcribing speech to text in real-time. It then analyzes this text for keywords specific to your chapter.

What happens after we sign up?

+

After signing up, our team will contact you to gather important information like your chapter's address, common names used for your house, and phone numbers for your exec team. We'll then set up your custom alert system within 48 hours.

Can we customize our alerts?

+

Absolutely! You can customize which keywords trigger alerts and who receives which types of notifications. We'll work with you to ensure you're only getting relevant alerts.

Is it only for fraternities?

+

While we originally designed Frat Alert for fraternities, we now proudly serve sororities and other campus organizations as well. The service works the same way for all groups.
