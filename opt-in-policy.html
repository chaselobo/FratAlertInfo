Opt-In Policy

Our Commitment to Consent

At Frat Alert, we believe in transparency and respect for individual privacy. Our opt-in policy ensures that all members receiving alerts have explicitly consented to be part of our notification system.

How Our Opt-In Process Works

When a chapter subscribes to Frat Alert, we implement a strict opt-in process:

  • Initial Setup: Chapter representatives provide a list of executive members who need to receive alerts.
  • Verification: Each member receives a verification text message requiring them to respond with "YES" to confirm their consent.
  • Ongoing Consent: Members can opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any alert message.
  • Periodic Renewal: Every semester, we request renewal of consent to ensure continued willingness to receive alerts.

Privacy Protection

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Phone numbers are stored securely and used exclusively for Frat Alert notifications.
  • We never share, sell, or use your contact information for marketing purposes.
  • All alert history is purged at the end of each semester to maintain data minimization.

Compliance with Regulations

Our opt-in policy complies with telecommunications regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and other applicable laws governing SMS communications.

By maintaining strict opt-in procedures, we ensure that our service respects both legal requirements and individual preferences.
